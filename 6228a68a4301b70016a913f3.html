Every product in the Martha Stewart Pets line is hand selected by Martha as she takes a practical approach to pet care, ensuring quality, function and innovation. Martha’s pet supplies for dogs embody these qualities and are here to make caring for your dog simple and stress-free. The Martha Stewart Pets Waste Bag Dispenser and Bags For All Dogs pouch is the perfect accessory to hold your dog waste bags. The dog waste bags dispenser is a super convenient way to reach your dog bags. The small trash bags fit snugly in the dog waste bag dispenser, so you can grab doggie bags whenever and wherever. The poop bags for dogs are leak-proof, so you don’t have to worry about dog poop leaking through the doggy bags. The poop bags are also tear-resistant, allowing for a fast and easy pick-up; you won’t have to worry about getting stuck on a walk with a torn dog poop bag. The poop bag dispenser comes in an adorable multi-colored pouch with a dog bone design, so you can have the cutest pet supplies for dogs. Directions:

1. Take your Martha Stewart pet bag from your dog bags for poop dispenser and pull it over your hand like a glove. Pick up waste with your bagged hand.

2. With your other hand, grasp open end of the poop bag and pull it inside out. The waste will now be inside the bag.

3. Gently tie and knot the end until completely closed. Dispose of safely.
