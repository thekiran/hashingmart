Manage account & common isuess with customer support

Live support is a help-desk resource that lets you conveniently manage refunds, returns, order related cases, other common account issues all in one place. Live support can be accessed from desktop and mobile devices, and offers a simple and intuitive interface so you can address issues without delay.

With Live support, you can:

  • Manage refunds, returns, and order related cases all in one place.
  • Submit requests to Hashingmart to review feedback and defects for removal.
  • Get help with account, shipping and billing issues
  • Report suspicious activities and actions that abuse our policies.
