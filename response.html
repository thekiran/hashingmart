Hashingmart's response to COVID-19

Hashingmart's response to COVID-19

COVID-19 has created unforeseen issues for us at Hashingmart and our shoppers around the world. We're following the situation closely and our community - buyers, sellers and employees, remain our top priority


On this page, you will find useful information on how we at Hashingmart are making this possible.


This page will be updated frequently - please check back for new information.


Getting customer service support


Customer service stays open at Hashingmart to serve despite the temporary service changes brought on by the Covid-19 impact. We are fully available and working on getting the telephone support available to you 24/7 even on weekends as well as holidays. There are also alternative ways for you to reach us whenever you need to:

  • If you are a buyer, our Chat support is easy-to-use for most enquiries or complaints. Here you can send a message help@hashingmart.com. Our team is working around the clock and will get back to you within 24 hours.
  • If you are a seller, our platform is relatively easy to use. Whether you have questions about listings and payments, or if you need to cancel or initiate a refund, your requests can be addressed quickly and efficiently here. Send a message to help@hashingmart.com and we will get back to you within 48 hours.

Thank you.

Should you need to report any item that violates our policies, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. Thanks for your patience as we strive to serve you better. We are doing everything in our power to help our community navigate these uncertain times. We ask that you be patient with us and treat each other with kindness. We are optimistic all of this will go away and we will come out stronger.
